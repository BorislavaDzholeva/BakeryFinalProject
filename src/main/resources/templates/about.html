About Us

We Bake Every Item From The Core Of Our Hearts

In the heart of Sofia, there is a place where real bread has been baked every day since 1987. Today, the bakery "Baking Love" brings the beautiful tradition of the bakery craft close to the capital city. A magical place where you can relive the joy of real bread.

Our bread is like good wine and is prepared according to old traditions. The ripening process combined with the right baking method results in bread full of flavor. The method we use guarantees really healthy and delicious bread every day. Each of the 20 types of bread has its own specific characteristics and taste. No matter what you choose – white or black, dietary, sourdough bread or traditional Bulgarian bread – you will get a real taste pleasure. Our bread is prepared without any additives and with a minimum amount of salt.

In the cozy atmosphere of "Baking Love" Bakery, you will be served with a good attitude and a smile, and of course you will receive suggestions and advice tailored to your wishes and needs. And all these delicacies have made us a favorite place for thousands of residents of the capital over the past 37 years.

You can find our bakeries at 37 Angel Kanchev St., at 1 Hristo Belchev St. or if you simply follow the tempting and unique aroma of freshly baked bread.
